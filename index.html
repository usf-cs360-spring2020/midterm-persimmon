Visualizing San Francisco Fire Department Calls for Service

For this project, we will use the Fire Department Calls for Service dataset from DataSF. Our focus is neighborhood data to visualize with respect to response time and quality of response from the fire department. Visuals will investigate a number of groups across neighborhoods including: unit type, station area, ALS unit, call type group, dispatch, response, and on scene time. We are interested in comparing dispatch times between San Francisco neighberhoods and determining whether certain neighborhoods receive faster dispatch as well as measuring how effective the responses are.
