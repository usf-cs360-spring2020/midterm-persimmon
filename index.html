Visualizing San Francisco Fire Department Calls for Service

For this project,we will use Fire SF dataset. We will focus on Neighborhoods, to visualize receive time of call with respect to response time from fire department. Visuals will investigate on a number of groups across neighborhoods including: unit type, station area, call type group, dispatch, response, and on scene time. Our visualizations will explore fire department response for various neighborhoods in San Francisco.

Learn more about the dataset by navigating to our data page. Learn more about our team by navigating to our about page
